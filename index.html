Since a young age, I have been mesmerized by computers and the limitless things you can create with them. Around this time, I also began gaming which only further boosted my curiosity about computers. I greatly love how games can be used to tell an interactive story and appreciate all genres. Some personal favorites include: Half-Life: Alyx, Life is Strange, Team Fortress 2, It Takes Two, Psychonauts, Hellblade: Senua's Sacrifice, Hades, The Forest, Garry's Mod, Minecraft, Bioshock Infinite and many more that, if listed, will fill this entire page.

While games are perhaps a more expressive way of demonstrating software development and programming, I strive to advance my knowledge in all areas of computer science.
Currently employed as a Software Engineer.
