Since a young age, I have been mesmerized by computers and the limitless things you can create with them. Around this time I also began gaming which only further boosted my curiosity about computers. I would play and wonder if some day I would be able to create something someone else would also enjoy this much.

I greatly love how games can be used as a way to tell an interactive story and appreciate all genres. Some personal favorites include: Half-Life: Alyx, Life is Strange, Team Fortress 2, It Takes Two, Psychonauts, Hellblade: Senua's Sacrifice, Hades, The Forest, Garry's Mod, Minecraft, Bioshock Infinite and many more that, if listed, will fill this entire page.
