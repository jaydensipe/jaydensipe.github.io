Let's take a look at our dataset...

The Video Game Dataset contains 20,580 entries, with each entry representing a video game. The dataset includes 8 columns:

  • id: Unique identifier for each video game.
  • name: Name of the video game.
  • released: The release date of the video game.
  • added: The number of times the game has been added or favorited.
  • playtime: The average playtime in minutes.
  • reviews_count: The number of reviews for the video game.
  • ratings_count: The total number of ratings received by the game.
  • rating: The average rating score for the video game.

What are the Top-10 Highest Rated Games? *

Here, you have the opportunity to explore the top-rated games from within this dataset*, enabling you to make informed decisions about which games to consider.

As you hover over each bar in the chart, you have the option to click and be directed to a YouTube video showcasing the trailer for the selected game. This interactive feature enables you to explore the game further and get a glimpse of it's gameplay.

Historically, which month released the most games?

This pie chart provides an overview of the number of games released in each corresponding month. By examining the chart, we can observe that November had the highest count, indicating the greatest amount of games released during that month.

To explore the exact total for each month, simply hover over a segment of the pie chart to reveal the cumulative count of games released in that particular month.
